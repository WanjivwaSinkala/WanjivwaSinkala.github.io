Project 2: Sustainability & Climate Resilience Initiative

This project focused on promoting sustainability and climate resilience through community-based interventions including reforestation, renewable energy adoption, and climate-smart agriculture programs.

Objectives:

  • Strengthen environmental resilience.
  • Promote sustainable livelihoods.

Methodology:

Participatory community engagement, technical training, and monitoring environmental impact.

Outcomes:

Increased tree cover, improved local agricultural practices, and enhanced community awareness.

Collaborations:

Worked with NGOs, banks, and local authorities to implement scalable, long-term solutions.
