Project 2: Sustainability & Climate Resilience Initiative

This project was carried out in collaboration with Standard Chartered Bank Zambia, Care International, FNB Zambia, and the Patients Aid Management Initiative (PAMZ). The focus was on reforestation and environmental stewardship to address the growing threats of deforestation and climate change.

Activities included community tree planting campaigns, sensitization workshops on sustainable land use, and support for local climate-smart practices. The initiative aimed to both restore degraded areas and build community awareness of the importance of environmental conservation for long-term resilience.

My contribution involved mobilizing community members, coordinating logistics, and monitoring progress of the tree planting efforts to ensure survival rates of saplings remained sustainable beyond the planting stage.
