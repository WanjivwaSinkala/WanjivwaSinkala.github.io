Project 1: Monitoring & Evaluation Framework Development

Project 1 involved designing and implementing a robust Monitoring & Evaluation framework for multiple development programs. The goal was to enhance accountability, track performance metrics, and improve program outcomes.

Objectives: Establish data-driven decision-making processes and performance tracking systems.

Methodology: Mixed-methods evaluation including surveys, interviews, and data analysis to measure impact.

Outcomes: Framework adopted by stakeholders, resulting in improved program efficiency and reporting accuracy.

Collaborations: Partnered with NGOs, government bodies, and local communities to ensure relevance and sustainability.
