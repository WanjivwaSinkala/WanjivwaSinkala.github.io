About Me

Introduction

I am Wanjivwa Sinkala, a Zambian development economist and operations professional committed to advancing institutional integrity, results-based practice, and inclusive growth. With over 7 years of experience across development, banking, retail, and legal sectors, I specialize in administration, procurement, compliance, and risk management.

Education

  • Master of Arts in Economic Policy Management – University of Zambia
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Monitoring & Evaluation Methods – University of Zambia
  • Bachelor of Arts in Development Studies with Economics – University of Zambia

Skills

  • Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)
  • Risk Management & Compliance
  • Procurement & Operations Management
  • Data Analysis & Reporting
  • Stakeholder Engagement & Coordination
  • Project and Policy Analysis

Values

I believe in accountability, transparency, and inclusive growth. My work is driven by the desire to promote financial inclusion, sustainable livelihoods, and capacity building for individuals and SMEs. Integrity and results-based practice are the pillars of my professional ethos.

Beyond Work

Outside of my professional commitments, I enjoy mentoring young professionals, contributing to community projects, and engaging in lifelong learning. I also participate in initiatives focused on environmental sustainability and financial literacy.
