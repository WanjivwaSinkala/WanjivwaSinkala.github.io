Project 3: Policy & Economics Initiative

This project focuses on providing actionable economic policy guidance to support inclusive growth and sustainable development. The initiative involved conducting in-depth data analysis, stakeholder consultations, and policy evaluations across key sectors.

Objectives: To improve evidence-based policymaking, strengthen institutional frameworks, and foster equitable economic growth.

Methodology: The project employed mixed methods including quantitative economic modeling, qualitative interviews with stakeholders, and comparative policy analysis.

Outcomes: Recommendations were successfully adopted by relevant ministries and agencies, contributing to policy reforms and improved governance structures.

Collaborations: Worked alongside government agencies, local NGOs, and international partners to ensure practical and sustainable solutions.
