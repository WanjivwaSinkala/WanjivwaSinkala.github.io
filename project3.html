Project 3: Policy & Economics Initiative

This project focused on providing actionable economic policy guidance to support inclusive growth and sustainable development. Activities included data analysis, stakeholder consultations, and policy evaluations across key sectors.

Objectives:

  • Improve evidence-based policymaking.
  • Foster equitable economic growth.

Methodology:

Mixed-methods evaluation, including quantitative modeling and qualitative interviews.

Outcomes:

Recommendations adopted by ministries and agencies, contributing to policy reforms.

Collaborations:

Worked with government agencies, NGOs, and international partners for sustainable solutions.
